**The Pythia at Delphi**
A dim stone chamber (the *adyton*) sunk below the temple floor. She sits on a bronze tripod straddling a fissure in the rock, pale vapor curling up around her ankles. **The trireme's rowing deck**
Three cramped tiers of rowers stacked almost on top of each other, oars threaded through ports in the hull. The ceiling is low, the air thick. Thin blades of sunlight stab through the oar-holes and stripe across sweating backs. At the center an *aulos* player pipes the stroke rhythm while the timekeeper calls out. It's claustrophobic, mechanical, almost industrial — very different texture from the marble-and-myth stuff.
**The Colossus of Rhodes, mid-construction**

**The oracle at Dodona**
