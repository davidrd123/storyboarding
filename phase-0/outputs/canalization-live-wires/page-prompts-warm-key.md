## Why this file exists

The source chat (`chats/2026-05-29_18-50-33_..._Important_vocabulary_rundown.md`) is **warm and generative, not bleak or ironic** — two minds building a structure and landing on something genuinely orienting. The earlier sepia/grimy comic pages inherited their gloom from the *reference comics* (Manson / Topanga), not from the material. This file re-pitches the canalization pages in the source's actual register.

**The brief for this line:**
- **Keep the zing, edge, and humor** — sourced from *delight*, not grievance. (The original nonna meme was funny; the problem was never humor, it was the *cynical* flavor.)
- **A positive influence to look at** — not a downer, but **not pure sunshine** either, and **not didactic**. The teeth stay (irreversibility, the warning against discharge) — they're just turned toward *agency* so the page orients rather than scolds.
- **Tone anchor:** an elder handing you a working instrument, not a ghost watching his tool get abused.
- **Palette anchor:** warm amber / gold / dawn, light coming *up*. Luminous woodcut, not midnight.

## GPT Image 2 notes / caveats (same as the companion file)

*Narrator: the man who drew the epigenetic landscape — quiet wonder, not grievance. He hands the reader a working practice. The "don't eat the dream" (charging vs. discharging) gold is folded into Panel 7 + the banner.*

**Watch (review pass):**
- **Keep the wink.** Panel 7's dream-eater is the one true visual gag — make sure it reads funny (sated, dopey, plate licked clean), so the page stays a comic, not a sermon. If the whole page reads earnest, that's the failure mode.
- **Didactic creep.** "LET ME SHOW YOU" + the mechanism rows can tip into lecture. The elder-handing-you-the-instrument framing is what saves it; if a panel feels like a textbook, cut the explainer line and let the image carry it.
- **Palette discipline.** Warm amber/dawn with light coming *up* — if codex's render comes back gloomy sepia, that's the borrowed-bleakness reflex; reroll toward luminous.
- **Lettering density.** 11 panels of text is at the edge of one-shot; verify spelling, gen in halves if needed.
