## Interpretation A - Codex compression, 2026-07-11

**Provenance:** Produced by Codex from the PDF's extracted text and embedded images when asked to create a compact agent handoff. ### Compressed creative brief

**Core proposition:** Peelerz lets you be creative. Eating or peeling the candy triggers exuberant transformations in people and the world around them.

**Narrative spine:** Three threads escalate and converge: Tina's car peels open and she parachutes away; Raul transforms into an anime mango Voltron while Meg watches; Liz and John connect on campus and drift through radically different worlds. Everyone finally meets among floating Peelerz islands above the clouds.

**Tone:** Joyful, surprising, weird, maximal, candy-colored, youthful, and romantically playful. The treatment explicitly invites changes of scale, transitions, flying, world-building, and strange visual gags.

**Practical constraint:** Each idea must read immediately and be expressible in a few strong visual styleframes.

### Ideation levers proposed in that compression

- Literalize `peel`: cars, environments, or reality itself can peel open.
- Play with scale: giant hands, giant doubles, enormous candies, miniature people, floating fruit islands.
- Use transformation as character expression: parachute freedom, anime power-up, romantic shared worlds.
- Build transitions around mouths, bites, peels, circular camera moves, explosive `BOOSH` changes, and match cuts.
- Let each flavor suggest its own material, physics, creatures, palette, and world.
- Preserve product visibility and a simple emotional read inside every spectacle.

### Starting points proposed in that compression

1. **Peel physics:** Define several visual rules for how reality peels - as gummy skin, fruit rind, packaging film, stage scenery, or layers of alternate worlds.
2. **Flavor universes:** Give each flavor a distinct genre and physical logic rather than treating every transformation as generic candy color.
3. **Escalation ladder:** Personal sensory change -> body or vehicle transformation -> environment change -> genre change -> shared sky-scale brand universe.
4. **Transition chain:** Bite explosion -> peel reveal -> airborne match cut -> anime energy -> mouth portal -> circular world swaps -> vertical ascent -> super-wide convergence.
5. **Character-specific creativity:** Tina chooses freedom/adventure; Raul gets playful power fantasy; Liz and John co-create romantic worlds. New ideas should reveal personality, not only spectacle.
6. **Styleframe test:** Every candidate should be understandable in one image, recognizable as Peelerz, emotionally legible, and distinct from the other beats.

### Claims added by the interpreter rather than stated directly

- The source does not explicitly say that peeling or eating determines every transformation.
- `Joyful`, `maximal`, `romantically playful`, `freedom`, `power fantasy`, and `co-create romantic worlds` are interpretive characterizations.
- `Read immediately`, flavor-specific physics, universal product visibility, and the proposed escalation ladder are additions rather than verbatim requirements.
- Treat these claims as hypotheses that may be challenged or replaced, not as constraints inherited from Ben's treatment.
