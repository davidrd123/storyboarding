# Next round — generation strategy

Text alone should not be expected to solve every design and continuity problem. Image references have helped substantially, and some missing authorities may be better created in Midjourney, by compositing existing images, or through another dedicated design pass before we return to scene generation.

## Separate reference problems from scene problems

### Can probably be improved with the references we already have

- Tina in the back seat and the initial product moment.
- Car larger than miniature tropical houses.
- Lychee helpers choreographing a pull or relay.
- Basic coverage choices: close product shot, exterior scale reveal, mechanical wide, launch angle.
- Meg actively engaging with the grape familiar.
- Public Raul spectacle versus private Meg interaction.
- Anime/levitation effects reaching the football game.

### Needs a stronger visual authority before more scene prose

- The transformed car's seam topology:
  - where the roof seam begins
  - how a front-to-back strip opens
  - how the sides divide into peel/petal sections
  - how the passenger compartment stays coherent
- The material transition from painted car surface to soft lychee rind/peel.
- The opened car peel becoming one functional parasail canopy.
- A truly specific mango-mecha design and silhouette.
- Opaque mango-flesh armor material rather than translucent gummy or orange superhero plastic.
- A daylight top-of-bleachers world plate that all Raul/Meg frames can inherit.
- Potentially a denser, less-translucent mango-helper turntable keyed to the real product still.

## Proposed staged workflow

### Stage A — make the missing design authorities in isolation

Do not start with full scenes. Make a small ingredient set first:

1. **Lychee peel-car seam sheet** — one dark sedan shown from top, side, and three-quarter angles with a clear seam map and broad peel/petal sections. This can be explored in Midjourney or assembled from existing car images.
2. **Car-peel-to-parasail silhouette progression** — three simple states: attached transformed roof skin -> unfurling pink canopy -> airborne lychee parasail. Prioritize silhouette and causality over character detail.
3. **Lychee crew action sheet** — the established tiny lychee helpers pinching, pulling, relaying, bracing, and riding one shared peel edge.
4. **Mango mecha design sheet** — specific front, three-quarter, side, and back views with readable mechanical joints, mango-flesh panels, and Raul's open face aperture. This is the highest-value Midjourney/manual-reference candidate.
5. **Mango material/helper sheet** — armor and helper material keyed to `peelerz/references/stills/97882-PeelerzGummy-Mango-Sample__43665.jpg`, less translucent and more appetizingly mango-like.

### Stage B — lock the worlds separately

1. Tina: approve one miniature tropical neighborhood plate with the car much larger than the houses.
2. Raul/Meg: create one daylight top-of-bleachers plate with empty space around them and the live football game below.

These plates become literal authorities for every later frame; do not redescribe the locations independently.

### Stage C — generate only the causal sequence beats

Use the isolated design sheets and world plates as references.

Tina:

1. Product/first intrusion.
2. Helpers create the first visible seam.
3. Car surface visibly changes into lychee peel before opening.
4. Crew pulls roof and side sections into a coherent flower/orange-peel shape.
5. The same material joins into a pink parasail and begins lifting Tina.
6. Upward launch frame, with the rainbow transition reserved for the next beat.

Raul:

1. Crew emerges from or activates the eaten mango peel.
2. Crew wraps Raul with one connected material.
3. Giant mango compression/replacement beat.
4. Mango opens into discrete referenced mecha components.
5. Pit crew completes specific mechanical connections.
6. Daylight hero reveal with Meg actively engaged.
7. Environmental payoff on the football game.

### Stage D — mutate, do not restart

- If a frame has the right composition but wrong material, use that frame as the edit target and change only the material.
- If the material is right but mechanics are wrong, preserve the frame and change only hand/peel contact and seam geometry.
- If the mecha is wrong, return to the isolated mecha authority rather than adding more adjectives to the stadium prompt.

## Practical split between tools

- **Midjourney or manual compositing:** strongest candidates for shape language, silhouettes, design sheets, seam topology, and the hero mecha concept.
- **Reference-guided Gemini scene generation:** best used after those ingredients exist, for continuity, character placement, world-plate inheritance, and variations of a locked beat.
- **Text-only exploration:** still useful for cheap discovery, but should not be treated as the final control mechanism for topology, material design, or multi-step causality.

## Stop condition before launching the full next batch

Do not run another large scene batch until we have at least:

- one usable lychee car-seam/peel authority;
- one usable peel-to-parasail authority;
- one usable mango-mecha authority;
- one daylight Raul/Meg world plate.

Once those four ingredients exist, the next scene round can be smaller, more coherent, and much easier to evaluate.

  - “lotus” became a literal giant flower;
  - “lighthouse” became a glowing-rope tableau;
  - “temporary joint” became a decorative candy chain;
  - “arc” became a freestanding rainbow gate.

  every replayed bite releases one mango-flesh component and affects the

  roof instead of transforming car skin.

  surfaces. This is a Midjourney/compositing candidate.
- Cute helpers are easiest to read mechanically in a dedicated insert. Asking
  one wide to show the entire mecha, all helpers, Meg, component paths, and the
  field causes the helpers to become decoration.

### Engine redesign after the image evidence

Focused visual-probe mode now includes a **Gate 2.5 render compiler**. Concept
seeds retain metaphor during divergence, but render prompts are compiled into:

- invariants;
- substrate;
- initial state;
- applied force and exact contact points;
- countable topology;
- visible state change;
- residue/attachment;
- minimum shot proof;
- explicit literalization hazards.

Metaphor and source-domain nouns are then removed from the prompt. A
three-mechanism A/B test confirmed that this materially improves obedience:

- removing flower/lotus language produced attached pink car-skin sheets rather
  than a flower mounted on the roof;
- Meg's connector action became fully readable, although the component design
  degraded into a mango-like loaf;
- five helpers, one open arm gap, attachment hardware, and an incoming hard
  connector survived into a close-up much more clearly than the figurative
  “living joint” version.

### Practical next split

- Use NB2 Lite for rapid micro-mechanism and continuity tests.
- Treat micro-mechanism shots and whole-spectacle shots as separate generation
  problems, then connect them editorially.
- Use Midjourney for high-value optical/material/aesthetic authorities: the
  reflection-first image, specific mango-mecha silhouette, appetizing mango
  panel design, and the decisive car-skin-to-parasail ingredient.
- Condensed Midjourney briefs are in
  `batch-18-focused-engine-good-weird/MIDJOURNEY-HANDOFF.md`.

## Open-canvas round 2 + Fable wild rounds — July 15

Three additional NB2 Lite batches broadened the Ben-facing range:

### What became visually productive

- **Material and geological laws:** flavor tides rebuild a city; repeated plays
  accumulate as turf strata; sunset becomes a liquid filling the street.
- **Clean spatial inversions:** the road flies instead of the car; islands come
  to Tina; the entire car encounter fits inside one gummy; the island is the
  opened candy.
- **Collective behavior:** Raul becomes a mango crowd wave; his giant form is a
  headcount; one missing helper stays with Meg; islands rearrange to welcome the
  approaching characters.
- **Deadpan one-channel substitutions:** an ordinary driver receives an exact
  full-size gummy replica of his car.
- **Finale as desire/attraction:** the separate encounter worlds curl inward as
  peel-scale geography and the islands actively form welcoming arms.

### What NB2 Lite still resists

- Multiple progressive realities in one frame become panels, white bays,
  triptychs, or duplicated characters.
- Simultaneous alternate football plays become floating screens instead of a
  coherent spatial volume.
- Complex subject/object inversions collapse into a literal prop: a pink hand,
  tail-like candy stretch, parachuting car, or decorative tableau.
- Four identity references in one frame only partially preserve the ensemble.
