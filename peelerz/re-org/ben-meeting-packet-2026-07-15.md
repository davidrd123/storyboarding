# Peelerz — Ben conversation packet

**Open this document for the conversation.** The longer visual board, prompt
guide, notes, and contact sheets are backup material only.

## What I want from this conversation

By the end, try to leave with four directional answers:

1. **Range:** How far beyond the current treatment should the creative
   situations go?
2. **Tina:** Do we keep solving the car-opening/parachute route, or replace it
   with a larger inversion?
3. **Raul + Meg:** Is the central pleasure an individual mango-mecha power-up,
   or a collective transformation involving the crowd and gummy characters?
4. **Finale:** Are the Peelerz Islands a destination waiting for everyone, or
   an active world that pulls the separate stories toward it?

## Opening framing — say this first

> I initially treated the embedded treatment images as destinations we needed
> to solve more precisely. Looking again at your notes, I realized the talent
> encounters and the Peelerz Islands finale are the fixed points, but the
> creative situations between them can be anything. So I brought two kinds of
> work: a grounded version that develops the current routes, and a deliberately
> wider set that tests how far the world itself can transform. These are visual
> probes, not a proposed edit. I mostly want to find the territory you want us
> to develop.

# 2. Tina — choose a trunk before solving mechanics

![Tina examines a lychee gummy in the back seat while gummy fish appear outside](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-09-grounded-mj-parallel/outputs/2026-07-14/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/01_01-interior-hero_21c58caf/01-interior-hero_01.png)

**Say:** “This intimate product moment still feels like the right entrance.”

## Current-treatment development: helpers open transformed car material

![A line of tiny lychee helpers pulls a seam across the car roof](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-11-tina-gummy-hands-region/outputs/2026-07-14/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/02_02-coordinated-roof-crew_b6739d8a/02-coordinated-roof-crew_01.png)

![A translucent pink canopy unfurls above Tina and the opened car](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-09-grounded-mj-parallel/outputs/2026-07-14/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/11_11-peel-unfurls-into-canopy_981799f2/11-peel-unfurls-into-canopy_01.png)

**Say:** “The grounded version is: small lychee helpers create seams in a car
surface that has already become peel-like; that same material unfurls into the
pink canopy and eventually the rainbow parasail.”

## Larger inversion: the destination comes to her

![Full tropical islands descend under enormous parachutes above Tina's cul-de-sac](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-22-fable-wild-round-a/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/11_11-islands-come-to-her_12060fd7/11-islands-come-to-her_01.png)

**Say:** “The reversal is that Tina does not need the familiar flight at all;
the islands descend into her neighborhood.”

## Ask Ben

> Is the pleasure here watching a precise car-to-canopy mechanism unfold, or
> should Tina's bite trigger a much larger reversal? If we keep the car route,
> do the gummy helpers feel essential or merely explanatory?

![Mango crew stretches around Raul while Meg joins in](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-16-raul-meg-locked-progression/outputs/2026-07-14/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/02_02-mango-crew-wraps-raul_ff5a1ca5/02-mango-crew-wraps-raul_02.png)

**Say:** “The current route gives Raul an individual power fantasy, with the
mango gummies acting as a visible pit crew rather than decorative particles.”

## Collective alternative: Raul becomes the crowd wave

![A crest of small mango gummy figures travels through the football crowd around Raul and Meg](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-23-fable-wild-round-b/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/01_01-raul-becomes-the-wave_4169bb4e/01-raul-becomes-the-wave_01.png)

**Say:** “A different route makes the event social: the gummies become the
stadium wave and Raul dissolves into its leading edge.”

## Power fantasy plus a small character choice

![A huge hand made of mango gummies rises behind Raul while one missing helper stays beside Meg](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-23-fable-wild-round-b/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/03_03-missing-minion_b10828a4/03-missing-minion_01.png)

**Say:** “This may be the cleanest combination of epic and cute: Raul gets the
giant formation, but one missing gummy chooses to stay with Meg.”

## Ask Ben

> Is Raul's transformation fundamentally about becoming a singular hero, or is
> it more surprising if he becomes part of a collective stadium event? How much
> should Meg actively cause, redirect, or participate in what happens?

# 4. Liz + John — keep their event relational

## Product action as the shared effect

![Liz and John stretch one luminous gummy between them on campus](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-23-fable-wild-round-b/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/06_06-shared-stretch-admission_a20edeac/06-shared-stretch-admission_01.png)

**Say:** “For Liz and John, the simplest useful direction may be to keep the
impossible action physically between them—the same two-layer gummy stretches
and bends the world before it snaps them into the next place.”

## The environment responds to their connection

![Liz and John remain on campus steps while whole university buildings walk across the grounds](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-20-ben-open-canvas/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/01_01-campus-buildings-migrate_d5e83d7a/01-campus-buildings-migrate_01.png)

**Say:** “The wider version lets the campus itself migrate while the step they
share remains the one fixed point.”

## Ask Ben

> Should their creative situation stay intimate and relational, or should the
> entire campus respond? Do they need the current mouth-dive structure at all?

# 5. Finale — destination or active desire?

## The island is physically inside the candy

![The ensemble stands on a colossal lychee fruit opening into a complete tropical lagoon world](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-22-fable-wild-round-a/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/12_12-island-is-the-candy_18c600ca/12-island-is-the-candy_01.png)

**Say:** “One reveal is that the Peelerz Island is literally the inside of a
colossal two-layer candy.”

## The worlds are pulled toward it

![A stadium, suburb, city, and campus curve inward as enormous peel segments around a tropical orange sea](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-23-fable-wild-round-b/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/09_09-all-four-worlds-lean-in_4838dbd7/09-all-four-worlds-lean-in_01.png)

## The islands actively welcome them

![Fruit-shaped islands rearrange into two enormous open arms around approaching parachutes](../ideation/visual-probes/batch-23-fable-wild-round-b/outputs/2026-07-15/gemini-31-flash-lite-image/11_11-islands-want-them_579a11f9/11-islands-want-them_01.png)

## Ask Ben

> Should the Peelerz Islands simply be the place everyone reaches, or should
> their desire for the characters be the force organizing the whole film?
