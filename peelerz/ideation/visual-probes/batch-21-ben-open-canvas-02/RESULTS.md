# Results — Ben open canvas, round 2

All six prompts completed with two NB2 Lite outputs each.

## Strongest

- **City rebuilt between flavor tides:** the clearest new world rule. The intact
  car and older submerged cities make the transformation readable at a glance.
- **Repeated plays become stadium geology:** a strong football-world idea and a
  useful alternative to transforming Raul. Variant 02 is cleanest; variant 01
  added unwanted labels.
- **Possible-days city:** visually rich and genuinely beyond the existing
  treatment, though it still reads somewhat like a contact sheet made physical.

## Useful but unresolved

- **Campus customs:** successive subtraction is legible, but NB2 turns the
  transitions into white architectural bays and duplicates the couple.
- **Attention selects the play:** the attention ribbons read, but the alternate
  fields become floating screens rather than a coherent stadium volume.
- **World-pod exchange:** the model makes a triptych-like spatial wipe. The idea
  may need separate before/mid/after shots or editorial assembly.

## Main learning

NB2 Lite handles a new **material or geological law** better than it handles
multiple ontological states in one frame. “Tides rebuild the city” and “plays
deposit turf strata” survive as coherent worlds; “six progressive realities”
tends to become panels, stages, or duplicated characters.
