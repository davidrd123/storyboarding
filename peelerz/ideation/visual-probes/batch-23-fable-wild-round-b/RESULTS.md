# Results — Fable wild round B adaptation

All eleven selected prompts completed with one NB2 Lite output each.

## Strongest Ben-facing probes

- **Raul becomes the wave:** the mango crowd physically travels through the
  bleachers; a promising collective transformation rather than armor.
- **The missing minion:** charming, specific, and unusually good at combining
  Raul's power fantasy with one gummy's small social choice.
- **Rebuilt better:** the deadpan full-size gummy sedan is instantly readable.
- **All four worlds lean in:** strong finale cosmology; the stadium and three
  other worlds curl around one orange sea as peel-scale geology.
- **The islands want them:** extremely clean aerial gesture—an archipelago
  rearranges into welcoming arms around approaching parachutes.
- **Queue at the sky:** a funny social consequence of the sky peeling, though
  it is more tableau than character story.

## Secondary / diagnostic

- Giant-by-headcount reads, although the face becomes a smooth oversized Raul
  mask instead of a complete gummy mosaic.
- Shared-stretch gets the gummy mechanism but changes John and loses some
  identity authority.
- New-fares has the right deadpan world but not a clear boarding action.
- Band-finally-in-tune failed literally and reverted to Raul and Meg sharing.
- Abstainer's-bite is a usable finale tableau but only partially inherits the
  full ensemble.

## Main learning

Collective behavior is a productive route to “good weird.” The strongest
images are governed by a simple social rule—**the gummies become the crowd
wave, one refuses the giant to stay with Meg, the islands lean toward the
arrivals**—rather than by generic spectacle alone.
