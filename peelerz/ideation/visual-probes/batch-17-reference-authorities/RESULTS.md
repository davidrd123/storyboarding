# Batch 17 — first-pass evaluation

![Batch 17 contact sheet](contact-sheet.png)

## 01 — lychee peel-car seam authority

Useful first seam map. The top view clearly establishes four large soft roof
sections and a lifted pink underside while preserving the sedan. It is weaker
as a complete topology authority because the side and rear views show only a
small roof corner rather than how the upper doors and sides unfurl. Use this as
the roof authority; make side-petal geometry the first targeted repair.

## 02 — car peel becomes parasail

The strongest result in the batch. The sequence reads immediately as petals ->
canopy -> flight, the lychee-pink material remains continuous, and the helpers
become a visible structural crew. It added Tina in the final state despite the
prompt, but that makes the causal idea more useful as a story reference. The
intermediate canopy remains attached to the car more literally than the desired
rear-seat cradle, which can be repaired during scene generation.

## 03 — lychee crew action authority

The pull, relay, brace, ride, and celebration actions are clear. Character
fidelity drifted from the established half-white/half-pink lychee helpers into
round raspberry-like figures. Use this as action and contact authority only,
not as final character design. A narrow mutation should restore the established
lychee body while preserving every pose and peel interaction.

## 04 — opaque mango-flesh mecha authority

Substantially better material and mechanical specificity than the earlier toy-
plastic/bodybuilder suit. Dense mango-flesh plates, graphite joints, locking
seams, and stable boots are legible across four views. The face is not Raul's
locked identity and the torso still retains some muscular anatomy. Use this as
the strongest current suit/material authority, with identity supplied separately
in scenes. A Midjourney or manual pass could push the silhouette farther from
human musculature if desired.

## 05 — mango helper material authority

Strong material correction: saturated, dense, softly fibrous, and only faintly
translucent. The turntable retains the irregular mango silhouette and simple
face, though the hands and feet are less functional than the action-sheet
version. Combine this material authority with the earlier hauling-pose authority.

## 06 — daylight top-bleachers world plate

Strong usable daylight reset. It preserves the isolated top-row composition,
large empty band of bleachers, field, crowd, and overall relationship of Raul
and Meg. Geometry and faces are regenerated rather than pixel-identical, but the
plate is coherent enough to serve as the next scene round's world authority.

3. Use frames 04–06 as the reference stack for a first daylight mango-mecha
