# Results — Raul & Meg paired pass

All 10 frames completed. Jobs 03 and 06–08 initially returned model-internal
errors and succeeded unchanged on retry.

## The useful hit

The public/private pairing reads. Raul's mango candy produces a loud social
event—helpers, assembly, synchronized posing, crowd energy—while Meg's green
candy produces a private character relationship. This feels like one authored
beat with two complementary appetites, not two unrelated transformations.

## Strongest material

- **01 — two candies wake differently:** perhaps the cleanest premise frame.
  Both candies contain agency, but their personalities are distinct before
  either transformation escalates.
- **02 — eat center / wear peel:** strong branded physical logic. Tiny helpers
  pull the long mango peel around Raul rather than summoning arbitrary armor.
- **05 — spectacle and private visitor:** the best pitch frame. The shallow
  focus composition makes Raul's public orange spectacle and Meg's intimate
  green encounter coexist without competing.
- **06 — Meg coaxes imp from gummy:** the strongest Meg-only image. It feels
  conspiratorial and character-driven rather than simply reactive.
- **07 — Meg accepts invitation:** the green ribbon begins to give the mouth
  transition intention, with Raul receding behind her.
- **09 — green candy passage:** the creature becomes a guide and the passage
  has a clear destination, making the transition feel motivated.

## What needs another pass

- **03** communicates assembly but turns the helpers into mango cubes and the
  limbs into block armor.
- **04** becomes a generic gummy strongman; the row of hype-men works better
  than Raul's finished form.
- **08** again fails to depict a true dive or camera-following geometry.
- **10** proves that both signatures can persist into the finale, but the image
  becomes broad fantasy illustration and changes Meg's styling.

For a compact Ben-facing styleframe set, the most coherent run is **01 → 02 →
05 → 06 → 07 → 09**. It samples an auspicious region around one resonant rule:
the gummies are characters with hands and motives, and their physical behavior
creates each person's transformation.
