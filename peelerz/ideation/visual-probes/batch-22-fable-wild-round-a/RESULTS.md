# Results — Fable wild round A adaptation

All thirteen selected prompts completed with one NB2 Lite output each.

## Strongest Ben-facing probes

- **Airborne asphalt lane:** direct, cinematic, legible, and much larger than a
  car transformation while retaining Tina's encounter.
- **Decanted sunset:** a beautiful whole-world material event; the image invents
  a pouring vessel, but the liquid-light street is strong.
- **The gummy contains the car:** excellent product-scale inversion and one of
  the cleanest single-frame ideas in the sweep.
- **Sun-peeled:** small and executable; sunlight visibly peels the gummy without
  helpers or magic effects.
- **Islands come to her:** reverses the expected journey cleanly and creates a
  direct bridge from Tina to the finale.
- **The island is the candy:** strong finale-scale image; a huge lychee opens as
  a flower-like landing world with the ensemble on its rim.

## Secondary / diagnostic

- Glass-jelly street, world-holds-its-breath, and decal-window are good quieter
  alternatives.
- Floating neighborhood became a parachuting car rather than a lifted town.
- Gummy-peels-the-world produced a literal pink hand and road coil.
- Candy-eats-the-stadium collapsed into a giant tail-like stretch.
- Bag-bigger-inside got the point of view but not the vast inner world.

## Main learning

Fable's inversions work best when they can be summarized as one concrete
spatial reversal: **the road rises, the islands arrive, the world fits inside
the candy**. More elaborate subject/object inversions still need shot splitting.
