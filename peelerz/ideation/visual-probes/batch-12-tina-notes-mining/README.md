[`../../mj-prompts/2026-07-14-04-tina-notes-mining.md`](../../mj-prompts/2026-07-14-04-tina-notes-mining.md).

with explicit reference-image roles, physical relationships, and invariants.

- Standing invariant: Tina remains in the back seat of the car
- Prompt source: [prompts.yaml](prompts.yaml)

## Reference strategy

- Original Tina-in-car frame: identity, wardrobe, product, car, and back-seat
  authority.
- Tina bite close-up: supporting face and lychee-gummy reference only.
- Original car-peels-open frame: peel and flight context only; its orange
  upholstery, rainbow styling, disconnected panels, and camera are rejected
  unless a job explicitly asks for separated stages.
- Successful miniature-neighborhood frame: used only where its palette or
  exterior-world treatment is relevant.
