# First-pass review

This concentrated search worked better than either maximum dispersion or
surface-level variation. The 12 frames remain one coherent conceptual family,
but they expose several meaningfully different ways to stage the family.

## Strongest cells

- **03 — pouch sends out gummy hands:** best causal connection. The product
  visibly releases the agents, who begin opening Tina's world from inside.
- **07 — giant hand with gummy assistants:** strongest combination of an
  established hit and a new ingredient. Human and gummy hands repeat the same
  peel gesture at two scales.
- **09 — gummy relay makes canopy:** turns the gummies into choreography rather
  than decoration and connects opening to the eventual lift.
- **10 — gummy tug opens car in two:** clean, symmetric, immediately readable,
  and funny. The action could be communicated in very few styleframes.
- **11 — airborne gummy chain:** a graceful bridge between peeling and flight;
  the hand-to-hand chain gives the peel a visible upward force.
- **12 — open-car gummy crew hero:** strongest resolved tableau and perhaps the
  most treatment-ready single image, although the arch should become a more
  clearly continuous canopy in another pass.

## Useful supporting cells

- **01 — rooftop duet:** establishes the smallest, simplest version of gummy
  agency. The peel shape needs clarification.
- **04 — monumental gummy:** memorable scale image and a useful alternate to a
  giant human hand, though the creature design is still amorphous.
- **05 — gummy fish crew:** good fusion of boundary intrusion and gummy agency;
  currently reads more as fish occupying the car than peeling it.
- **06 — miniature neighborhood crew:** strong comic world and scale contrast;
  the swarm overwhelms the specific peel action.

## Misses

- **02 — coordinated roof crew:** communicates the crew but not their relay;
  the loose rectangle reads as an unrelated sheet.
- **08 — gummy underside self-peels:** the material becomes an animal-like pink
  body, losing the clean confectionary-hand idea and approaching body horror.

## What the machinery contributed

The useful output was not the number of variants. It was coverage of structural
possibilities inside a promising region:

- product causes agents;
- small agents collaborate with a giant agent;
- agents choreograph the peel;
- the peel action generates lift;
- the family resolves into a hero composition.

The next mutation pass should stay around **03, 07, 09, 10, 11, and 12**, with
one targeted correction per cell. There is little reason to keep expanding the
region until those stronger structures are tested more cleanly.
