# July 15 visual probes — prompt guide

Use this as the readable map for the three newest NB2 Lite batches.

## Where the exact prompt lives

- **`scenes.yaml`** is the easiest place to read every full prompt in a batch,
  in contact-sheet order. It also shows the reference images and shared system
  prompt.
- **`job.json`** records the exact resolved prompt and references used for one
  particular output. Use it when an image surprises us or appears to contradict
  the batch prompt.
- **`RESULTS.md`** is our interpretation after seeing the images: what worked,
  what failed literally, and what to carry forward.

## Fastest review route

1. Scan a contact sheet.
2. Find its row here to learn what we were asking for.
3. Read `RESULTS.md` to see whether we think the image obeyed.
4. Open that output's `job.json` only when the exact prompt/reference record is
   important.
