# Results — locked Raul & Meg progression

The round produced 34 scene variants plus six targeted corrections. The
`selects/` directory contains one editorially useful choice per prompt in story
order; it is a curation, not an assertion that every selected frame is final.

## What materially improved

### The top-row staging

The world plate was the most consequential decision. Empty rows give Raul's
event room to grow, preserve the football setting below, and make Meg's encounter
feel like private theater. The generated scene now has usable geography instead
of a generic crowd backdrop.

### Raul's causal sequence

Frames **01–04** now read as a real product-driven progression:

1. the center is eaten and little mango workers wake inside the empty peel;
2. they pull that same peel around Raul;
3. Raul is completely replaced by a giant mango gummy;
4. pressure and lightning build beneath its stretched surface.

The giant-mango swap and stress insert are especially clean. Frame **05** makes
the shell open into armor while retaining the helpers, so the transformation has
physical causality rather than arbitrary orange effects.

The unresolved part is finished-form design fidelity. The corrections produced
clearer hard-surface paneling, but the model still gravitates toward orange
superhero armor instead of the more specific industrial mecha sheet. The
sequence idea is proven; a final mecha styleframe would benefit from a stronger
approved suit comp or a dedicated design pass rather than more scene prose.

### Grape Meg

Grape is visually stronger in this pass than green apple. The deep violet is
distinct from Raul's orange without reading as a generic acid-green mascot. More
importantly, the peel becomes the familiar's hood and cape, so the creature is a
product transformation rather than an unrelated imp.

Strongest Meg frames:

- **09:** the distant referee/anime gag while she studies the grape;
- **10:** public orange spectacle versus private violet visitor;
- **11:** the familiar physically emerging as the peel becomes its hood;
- **14:** the first dive render with unmistakable empty air on both sides;
- **15:** violet confectionary passage continuously becoming campus ivy.

The bow correction enlarged the familiar and harmed scale, so the curated set
keeps the quieter original private-theater frame. The invitation frame is useful
but its long candy ribbon comes close to reading as tongue-like; it should not be
the hero image.

### Alternates

- **16, grape bunch:** charming evidence that grape multiplicity could work,
  though it creates a different Meg story from the single familiar.
- **17, gummy crowd:** an immediate comic image and one of the strongest wild
  alternates. It preserves the teenagers and geography while transforming only
  the lower spectators.

## Useful workflow lessons from Patrick's workspace

The files in `ComfyPromptByAPI-patrick/WorkingSpace/patrick` changed the round in
four concrete ways:

- **Character turntable before scenes:** front/three-quarter/side/back reference
  coverage reduced independent redesign across frames.
- **Style lock in isolation:** the gummy characters were resolved on neutral
  backgrounds before stadium compositing.
- **World plate as authority:** later prompts continued one approved wide rather
  than redescribing a bleacher world seventeen times.
- **Mutation prompts as narrow deltas:** corrections preserved successful
  compositions better than full prompt rewrites, while also revealing when a
  design change was too large for a simple mutation.

The best Ben-facing core is **01 → 03 → 04 → 05 → 09 → 10 → 11 → 14 → 15**.
Frames **16–17** belong after it as optional idea cards.
