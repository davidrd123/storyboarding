# MJ visual ideation — 08: gummy-guys register (from inspo/gummy-guys)

**Date:** 2026-07-14
**Source:** peelerz/ideation/inspo/gummy-guys/ — David's curated board.
**Register correction vs batch-15:** less face, more fruit. Faces are
two dots + tiny mouth + blush; limbs are stubs or doodles; character
stays ~90% fruit. Materials law: matte sugar-frosted SKIN over glossy
translucent FLESH; the peel reveals a contrasting inside color
(product-true: dual layer, both edible).
**For NB adaptation:** use the inspo folder images directly as refs —
grape-chicks watercolor for the Meg familiar, onigiri hill + Ghibli
cluster for crew behavior, doodle-limb mango for the hybrid register.
Singles below are 1:1 for clean ingredient crops.

### 1. Mango guy, minimal (sticker-flat)

```
a small mango character that is almost entirely mango, plain fruit silhouette with a tiny green leaf, stubby little arms and legs, a face made of two dots and a small open mouth with blush cheeks, matte sugar-frosted candy skin, soft flat illustration on plain cream background, sticker style --ar 1:1
```

### 2. Mango guy, photo-doodle hybrid

```
a photograph of a real ripe mango with crude hand-drawn black marker stick arms and legs and a simple doodled face with two dots and blush cheeks, standing on a plain white background, mixed media, the fruit stays photorealistic --ar 1:1
```

### 3. Mango crew behavior (multiplicity gag)

```
a dozen identical small mango gummy characters with tiny stub limbs and dot faces tumbling and sliding down a bleacher step like kids on a hill, one at the bottom sitting up happily, gentle physical comedy, soft flat illustration, plain background --ar 16:9
```

### 4. The grape familiar (grape-chicks logic)

```
a small pale green translucent jelly creature wearing its own peeled violet grape skin as a pointed hood and little cape, tiny dot eyes and a sly small mouth, glossy candy flesh under matte frosted purple skin, standing on a plain white background, watercolor and ink illustration --ar 1:1
```

### 5. The bunch (cluster as crowd)

```
a bunch of deep violet grapes where a few grapes have peeled open revealing small pale green jelly chicks inside, one chick wearing its violet skin as a hood, the rest of the bunch intact and glossy, plain white background, watercolor and ink illustration --ar 1:1
```

### 6. Lychee guy (materials law demo)

```
a small round lychee character with bumpy rose-pink sugar-frosted skin peeled halfway down like a jacket, revealing a pearly translucent white jelly body with a tiny dot face and a faint dark seed glowing inside like a heart, plain white background, soft illustration --ar 1:1
```

### 7. Mango parrot (photoreal fruit-animal)

a photorealistic small plump tropical parrot whose body is a ripe mango, yellow-red fruit gradient for feathers, a green leaf over its head, real bird feet and beak, perched on a suburban power line, bright daylight, seamless photo composite --ar 16:9
