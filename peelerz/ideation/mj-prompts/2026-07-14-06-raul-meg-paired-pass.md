# MJ visual ideation — Raul & Meg, paired pass

**Date:** 2026-07-14  
**Purpose:** give Raul and Meg two distinct but interlocking imaginative events
inside the same football-bleacher encounter.

## Resonant structure

- Raul's event is public, orange, explosive, and socially witnessed.
- His mango gummy helpers are an energetic hype crew who physically construct
  his Ani-Mango form.
- Meg's event is private, acid-green, intimate, and chosen.
- A single sardonic green-apple gummy imp invites Meg into the mouth passage,
  giving the transition an agent and giving Meg an event of her own.
- Rhythm: Raul peaks → Meg notices her visitor → Meg accepts its invitation →
  the camera follows.
