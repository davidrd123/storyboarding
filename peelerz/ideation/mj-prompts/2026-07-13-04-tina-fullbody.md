# MJ visual ideation — Tina round 03: the handmade suburb, full-bodied

**Date:** 2026-07-13
**Correction absorbed:** prompts carry the content here (no curated sref
board doing the style work) — set materials, light design, camera all
in the text.
**Reading of David's direction:** Gondry-lineage music video — handmade
set, visible trick, gel lighting; transformation arrives as the set
coming alive. Fork offered: Gondry mode (artifice celebrated) vs
sealed tableau mode (Anderson-adjacent).

## 1. The full look — Gondry mode

```
a teenage girl with wavy dark hair sits in the back seat of a real 1990s sedan parked in the middle of a handmade miniature suburb, waist-high houses built from painted cardboard and balsa wood with warm tungsten bulbs glowing in their tiny windows, felt lawns, paper palm trees with curling crepe-paper fronds, a hand-painted teal sky backdrop with visible brushstrokes, translucent pink gummy fish hung on fishing line at staggered depths slowly turning, thin haze catching a hot-pink gel key light from camera left and a teal rim light from behind, shot on 35mm Kodak film with soft anamorphic flares, the charm of a Michel Gondry music video where the trick is proudly visible --ar 16:9
```

## 2. The before — the set asleep, one pink spot

```
a handmade miniature suburb asleep under flat gray work lights on a soundstage, dust sheets draped over half the cardboard houses, cables and C-stands visible at the edge of the set, a real sedan parked among the tiny homes with a bored teenage girl reading in the back seat, everything in desaturated putty and concrete tones except one hot-pink followspot punching through thin haze onto her hands as she holds a small pink candy, the only saturated color in the frame, 35mm film grain, music video production still --ar 16:9
```

## 3. Every house its own gel — the flavor palette as lighting

```
night on a miniature suburban street built at knee height, each little cardboard house glowing from within in a different candy color, lychee pink, mango amber, acid green, deep grape violet, colored light spilling across felt lawns and pooling on miniature asphalt, a full-size car creeping down the too-small street with the only white headlights in the scene, camera low at the rooftop height of the tiny houses, haze, anamorphic lens flares, 35mm music video look --ar 16:9
```

## 4. The crane reveal — authored world

```
high overhead crane shot of a glowing tabletop suburb surrounded by the darkness of a soundstage, lighting rigs and silhouetted crew just visible at the edges of the miniature world, at its center a real car lit pink with a girl visible through the rear window, translucent pink gummy fish suspended on lines above the set like a slowly turning mobile, beams of haze light crossing the dark, 35mm film, the reveal shot of a music video --ar 16:9
```

## 5. The full look — sealed tableau mode (the fork)

```
perfectly symmetrical wide shot of a teenage girl in the back seat of a parked pastel sedan, centered in a meticulous miniature suburb that is unmistakably a set, flat frontal lighting, hand-painted teal sky, rows of identical cardboard houses in sorbet colors, pink gummy fish hanging in a precise grid overhead, deadpan storybook composition, Wes Anderson tableau crossed with a candy commercial, 35mm film --ar 16:9
```
