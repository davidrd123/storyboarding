# MJ visual ideation — Tina, grounded runs

**Date:** 2026-07-14  
**Purpose:** use the useful part of the creativity machinery without letting it
invent a new film. The story is locked; only the visual treatment varies.

## Locked story facts

- Tina is in the back seat of the car with the pink lychee gummy and pouch.
- The world outside can become tropical, colorful, and slightly miniature.
- A few gummy fish can cross from the outside world into the car as a visual
  rhyme and a light underwater foreshadow.
- The sequence resolves through one of two existing mechanics only:
  1. a giant hand peels the car open;
  2. the car peel becomes a parachute.
- No new causal system, floating-island world, visible soundstage reveal, or
  extra plot beat.

## What to vary

Use the machinery only to make combinations from these bounded visual axes:

- **Light:** warm sun / cyan fill / pearly pink bounce / dusk / night gels
- **Miniature material:** painted balsa houses / paper palms / felt lawns /
  molded-plastic suburb
- **Camera:** intimate back-seat close-up / view through the side window /
  exterior wide / low angle / overhead
- **Change level:** almost ordinary / color arriving / fish crossing the glass /
  pre-peel / full peel

For reference runs, pin the existing Tina image as the character or omni
reference. Add one mood still as an image prompt or style reference. Treat the
references as ingredients, not as an edit request. Keep stylization low enough
that MJ follows the physical action.

## Running rule

Generate one grid per prompt. Keep at most two images from a grid. Mutate the
keeper by changing only one allowed visual axis at a time. If an output invents
a new story mechanic, treat it as a miss even when it is beautiful.
