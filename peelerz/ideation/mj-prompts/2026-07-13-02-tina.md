**Date:** 2026-07-13
**Trigger:** David's reaction to batch-04-lite render

change outside; gummy fish inside the car (outside leaking in); pink
pickup from the lychee gummy; underwater foreshadow.

**Devices named from that frame (transferable):**
- **Leak-in:** the transformed outside sends small tokens into the car
  before the car itself peels — a middle step in the outside-in build.
- **Flavor-color propagation:** the world changes to HER flavor's
  color (lychee pink). Generalizes: each character's flavor tints
  their beat (Meg acid-green, Raul mango-gold).
- **Air-as-water pre-echo:** suspended drifting fish make air read
  aquatic; foreshadows the couple's underwater world from beat one.
- **Handmade register:** photoreal girl vs miniature stop-motion
  world; visible strings = authored world (feeds Tina-as-dreamer).
