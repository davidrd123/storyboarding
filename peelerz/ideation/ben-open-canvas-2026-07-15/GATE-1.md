# Gate 1 — Ben open-canvas round

The gate compares developmental potential and visual discussion value. It does
not reward fidelity to the current treatment destinations.

## Render set

The six NB2 Lite probes are W2, W3, M3, R4, G2, and G3. They span:

- external world transformation;
- time transformation;
- medium/authorship transformation;
- inside/outside and scale exchange;
- mythic sports world-building;
- full cosmological expansion.

M2 and W4 remain deliberately unrendered reserves because their value may be
better shown through paired frames or motion rather than forcing them into one
poster.
