# Focused creative-engine pilot — divergence trace

External entropy is reproducible with seeds 1701–1706. No draw was rerolled.
The six configurations for each focus were routed into two adjacent, two
lateral, and two feral seeds according to the amount of inherited scene logic
they necessarily break.

## Focus A — Tina: opening becomes lift

CONCERN: Tina's escape fantasy should grow from a visibly product-specific
transformation.

GAP: previous renders either tear metal, produce an unrelated canopy, or hide
the car-to-parasail causality.

TRUNK: lychee crew creates seams; transformed car material unfurls and becomes
the first pink parasail.

INHERIT: Tina identity and back-seat position; dark sedan; lychee flavor;
miniature tropical neighborhood; benign invitation.

MAY MUTATE: opener, topology, material transition, lift physics, scale, medium,
and presence of additional Tinas.

### T1 — adjacent — sugar-frost lotus rig

DRAW: coordinated lychee rigging crew / four lotus petals / sugar frost traces
seams / shell joins into parasail / miniature music-video tableau. Oblique:
“Change instrument roles.”

RULE: The lychee helpers do not pull the car open; they pull glowing sugar-
frost seam lines, and the softened car surface follows those lines into four
petals that join above Tina as a parasail.

VISUAL PROOF: A car twice the height of the miniature houses; Tina centered in
the intact back seat; tiny helpers hauling luminous seams rather than panels;
four pink-lined petals halfway between roof and canopy; delighted recognition;
wide miniature tableau.

BRIDGE: PEEL/LAYER + escape. The crew changes roles from laborers moving matter
to musicians “playing” tension lines that conduct the peel.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + oblique interruption / config 2 + “Change
instrument roles” / trunk.

### T2 — adjacent — giant-hand kite strip

DRAW: giant human hand / one front-to-back roof strip / miniature stage-set
material / peel catches air like a kite / surreal fashion film. Oblique: “Give
way to your worst impulse.”

RULE: Once the car ripens into a flexible miniature prop, one giant hand peels
its entire roof front-to-back in a single satisfying strip; the strip catches
air and lifts Tina's rear seat like a kite tail.

VISUAL PROOF: Low passenger-side wide; giant fingers pinch one clear leading
edge; a single blue-backed, pink-lined roof strip arcs upward; Tina and her
seat remain attached beneath it; tiny model houses emphasize scale; elegant
fashion-film calm rather than destruction.

BRIDGE: PEEL + escape. The deliberately excessive “whole roof in one pull”
impulse becomes the cleanest readable physical action.

LINEAGE: explicit mutation + oblique interruption / config 4 + “Give way to
your worst impulse” / giant-hand trunk.

### T3 — lateral — three-Tina wing release

DRAW: duplicate Tinas / accordion gills / colored light softens panels / roof
becomes two wings / impossible documentary photograph. Oblique: “Ask your
body.”

RULE: Colored light makes both car sides breathe open like accordion gills;
two temporary Tinas brace the gills until they become wings, then the central
Tina rises between them.

VISUAL PROOF: One documentary-wide frame of the giant car among tiny houses;
the central Tina remains in the rear seat while two duplicates lean bodily
against symmetrical pink gills; the upper folds have already become broad
wings; bodily effort is comic and legible.

BRIDGE: LAYER + autonomy. Her possible selves physically make room for the
self who leaves.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + personal analogy / config 1 + “Ask your
body” / doppelgänger trunk.

### T4 — lateral — shadow pushes the inside out

DRAW: car shadow / lotus petals / black upholstery becomes peel / gummy-carried
hammock / stop-motion choreography. Oblique: “Remove ambiguities and convert to
specifics.”

RULE: The car's shadow climbs onto the body and pushes the black rear upholstery
out through four sugar-bright seams; the seats become pink-lined petals that
the lychee crew carries upward as Tina's hammock.

VISUAL PROOF: Stop-motion side tableau; a flat black shadow with visible hands
presses from beneath the car; four seat-colored petals emerge cleanly without
torn metal; Tina sits in the center hammock while helpers take fixed positions
along its edge.

BRIDGE: INSIDE/OUTSIDE + escape. The interior literally becomes her vehicle
out, and every agent's contact point is visible.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + oblique interruption / config 3 + “Remove
ambiguities and convert to specifics” / upholstery-transform trunk.

### T5 — feral — the reflection peels first

DRAW: car shadow / lotus petals / reflected car changes first / peel becomes a
kite / impossible documentary photograph. Oblique: “Do nothing for as long as
possible.”

RULE: Tina and the real car remain perfectly still while the car reflected in
every window and puddle blooms open first; only when the reflected peel catches
wind does it pull the real roof after it like a kite string.

VISUAL PROOF: Tina motionless in the back seat; ordinary car still intact;
passenger window and wet street reflection show the same car already opened
into four pink petals; one reflected strip crosses the glass boundary and tugs
the real roof corner.

BRIDGE: REVEAL + curiosity/escape. The world demonstrates the hidden action
before reality consents to it.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + oblique interruption / config 5 + “Do
nothing for as long as possible” / reflections-transform-first trunk.

### T6 — feral — gummy zipper / giant pull

DRAW: giant hand / zipper seam drawn by moving gummies / sugar frost softens
surface / peel catches air like a kite / anime insert in live action. Oblique:
“Abandon normal instruments.”

RULE: Tiny lychee helpers become living zipper teeth across the sugar-frosted
roof; the giant hand pulls the last helper like a zipper tab, converting the
car surface into one airborne kite strip behind it.

VISUAL PROOF: Live-action wide with a sharp anime action insert around the
roof; helpers interlock hand-to-hand along one diagonal seam; a giant hand lifts
the leader; behind the moving line rigid blue car becomes flexible pearly-pink
peel; Tina laughs below.

BRIDGE: PEEL/STRETCH + playful escape. The opener is made from the product's
own little characters rather than a mechanical tool.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + oblique interruption / config 6 + “Abandon
normal instruments” / roof-crew trunk.

## Focus B — Raul: cute labor becomes specific power

CONCERN: Raul's public power fantasy needs clear assembly causality, mango
material, and active Meg participation.

GAP: scene prose produces orange bodybuilder armor; helpers decorate rather
than construct; Meg watches from outside the mechanism.

TRUNK: crew -> giant mango -> components -> pit crew -> mango mecha.

INHERIT: Raul and Meg identities; daylight top bleachers; mango versus grape;
opaque mango-flesh mecha; cute helpers; friends/seatmates.

MAY MUTATE: foreman, formation logic, sports spectacle, Meg's role, crowd and
field behavior.

### R1 — adjacent — bleachers become the assembly drawing

DRAW: giant mango shell / continuous peel locks into panels / anime instant /
grape familiar directs / bleachers fold into assembly diagram. Domain: volcano
observatories.

RULE: When the giant mango opens, the empty bleacher rows tilt into a precise
section diagram; Meg's grape familiar reads the glowing “fault lines” and waves
each peel-panel into its matching place on Raul.

VISUAL PROOF: Daylight wide from lower bleachers; aluminum rows form a giant
exploded-view grid behind Raul; dense mango panels hover along those lines;
small grape familiar directs traffic from Meg's palm; Meg turns with it,
actively following the build.

BRIDGE: LAYER + witnessed power. The bleachers become a monitoring instrument
for the pressure inside the mango shell.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + synectic analogy / config 2 + volcano
observatories / locked progression trunk.

### R2 — adjacent — mango flower pit stop

DRAW: mango pit crew / giant mango opens into pre-shaped petals / mechanical
flower / Meg holds connector / bleachers become assembly diagram. Domain:
lighthouse keeping.

RULE: The giant mango blooms into five pre-shaped armor petals; each petal can
lock only while Meg holds one glowing peel connector taut like a lighthouse
beam and the pit crew guides the matching panel home.

VISUAL PROOF: Open daylight composition with Raul centered inside a five-petal
mango machine; eight helpers visibly push panels; Meg braces both hands around
one long luminous connector from her seat; the connector points to the next
joint; field and empty rows remain legible.

BRIDGE: PEEL/LAYER + friendship inside public power. Meg's steady signal makes
the spectacle navigable.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + synectic analogy / config 3 + lighthouse
keeping / giant-mango and pit-crew trunks.

### R3 — lateral — every bite is a replayed component

DRAW: grape familiar foreman / each bite becomes a component / sports replay /
Meg receives matching piece / field levitates. Domain: pipe-organ tuning.

RULE: Each bite Raul took reappears on the scoreboard as a gummy replay; at
the exact chew, one mango-flesh component sounds into place and the matching
football formation rises one foot off the field.

VISUAL PROOF: Over-shoulder wide from Raul and Meg; scoreboard shows a giant
close-up bite with gummy freeze-frame marks; one dense shoulder component flies
out of the screen toward Raul; players below hover in formation; the grape
familiar hands Meg a small matching wrist component.

BRIDGE: CHOOSE/BITE + recognition. The crowd sees his private product action
become a public build, tuned one component at a time.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + synectic analogy / config 1 + pipe-organ
tuning / product-activation trunk.

### R4 — lateral — halftime silhouette extrusion

DRAW: grape familiar foreman / armor extrudes from Raul's silhouette / marching
band drill / Meg receives component / active play becomes anime. Domain:
marathon pacing.

RULE: The marching band's formation traces Raul's shadow across the field;
every completed measure raises one mango-flesh layer out of his silhouette,
paced so Meg receives the final piece exactly as the football play turns anime.

VISUAL PROOF: High daylight wide; Raul's enormous flat shadow reaches the field;
band members occupy its contour like drafting points; raised orange relief
plates travel back up the shadow toward him; Meg catches one small panel; one
runner at frame edge has just gained anime speed lines.

BRIDGE: REVEAL/LAYER + being witnessed. His latent heroic outline becomes a
public halftime image one paced section at a time.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + synectic analogy / config 4 + marathon
pacing / anime-game trunk.

### R5 — feral — mango relief glacier

DRAW: pit crew / armor extrudes from silhouette / trophy ceremony / Meg lifted
to eye level / crowd becomes fruit gummies. Domain: glacier monitoring.

RULE: Raul's shadow thickens into a slow mango-flesh relief glacier; helpers
plant tiny marker flags at each advancing armor edge, and when it reaches his
body the gummy crowd raises Meg on a trophy platform to inspect him eye-to-eye.

VISUAL PROOF: Daylight impossible documentary wide; a raised orange topographic
shadow flows up the empty bleachers into Raul's legs; tiny mango surveyors mark
its leading edge; lower fruit-gummy spectators lift Meg on a small silver seat;
Raul's upper body is already mecha.

BRIDGE: LAYER/MINIATURE + recognition and friendship. The absurd monitoring
ritual makes slow material accumulation visible.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + synectic analogy / config 5 + glacier
monitoring / shadow and gummy-crowd alternates.

### R6 — feral — helpers are temporary joints

DRAW: giant mango shell / helpers link bodies into temporary joints / Formula
One pit stop / grape familiar directs / crowd becomes gummies. Domain: swarm
robotics.

RULE: The opened mango panels cannot touch at first; teams of helpers link
hand-to-hand between them as temporary living elbow and knee joints, then peel
away into a victory crew only after the graphite connectors lock.

VISUAL PROOF: Low daylight pit-lane angle along the bleacher row; Raul stands
inside dense mango armor with visible gaps; chains of tiny mango helpers bridge
each joint under tension; Meg's grape familiar signals the lock; behind them
the fruit-gummy crowd waves like a coordinated swarm.

BRIDGE: STRETCH/LAYER + power supported by belonging. Cute characters are
literally load-bearing before the machine can stand alone.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + synectic analogy / config 6 + swarm
robotics / pit-crew trunk.

## Focus C — cross-scene release rhyme

CONCERN: Tina and Raul should feel part of one escalating film without sharing
the same transformation.

GAP: current scenes have separate effect vocabularies; levitation is only a
loose thought.

TRUNK: Tina releases upward; Raul assembles publicly; a repeated airborne rule
or transition object can connect them.

INHERIT: Tina car world; Raul daylight bleachers; distinct flavors and
characters; live-action commercial register.

MAY MUTATE: gravity, color, peel continuity, match cut, shadows, scale, crowd.

### C1 — adjacent — the discarded peel conducts color

DRAW: color travels on one strip / pink parasail rises as car settles / players
hover / peel ribbon crosses cut / tactile miniatures. Constraint: discarded
part is protagonist.

RULE: The discarded pink peel ribbon exits Tina's frame under tension, crosses
the cut, and arrives orange along a football field stripe; wherever it passes,
people become weightless for one second.

VISUAL PROOF: Matched two-frame pair dominated by one continuous diagonal
strip; Tina and the car are small behind the rising ribbon; in the next frame
the orange strip snakes between hovering players toward Raul; characters are
background to the traveling material.

BRIDGE: PEEL/STRETCH + release. The discarded layer becomes the film's active
conductor.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + constraint transformation / config 1 +
“discarded part is protagonist” / Tina launch and football levitation trunks.

### C2 — adjacent — canopy arc becomes scoreboard arc

DRAW: color strip / pink parasail rises / field lines lift / rainbow canopy
becomes scoreboard arc / quiet awe. Constraint: works with sound off.

RULE: As Tina's pink canopy gains rainbow color, its curved edge crosses frame
and becomes the scoreboard arch; the same colors then descend as raised field
markings that outline Raul's assembly space.

VISUAL PROOF: Two silent, composition-matched wides: canopy arc fills Tina's
upper frame; identical scoreboard arc fills Raul's; colored yard lines stand
upright around him like an open construction drawing.

BRIDGE: REVEAL/LAYER + awe. Color is a visible baton, not an extra magical law.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + constraint transformation / config 3 +
“beat must work with sound off” / parasail and world-plate trunks.

### C3 — lateral — the peel becomes the next sky

DRAW: discarded peel becomes next sky / roof becomes horizon / players hover /
peel ribbon crosses cut / tactile miniatures. Constraint: impossible thing
obeys one real physical law perfectly.

RULE: The roof peel rises only because moving air inflates it; once it fills
Tina's frame it becomes the entire daylight sky above the stadium, where its
continued upward draft lifts the football formation below.

VISUAL PROOF: First frame looks upward past Tina into the inflated pink roof;
second frame reveals the same membrane as a vast pale stadium sky with players
hovering in the shape of the previous canopy ribs; air-flow direction remains
physically consistent.

BRIDGE: PEEL + escape becoming shared awe. One real buoyancy rule spans both
worlds.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + constraint transformation / config 2 +
“one real law perfectly” / canopy trunk.

### C4 — lateral — the helper crosses alone

DRAW: color travels on one strip / Tina rises / Meg remains grounded while all
else rises / floating helper crosses / quiet awe. Constraint: product never
shown whole.

RULE: One tiny lychee helper loses its grip on Tina's moving peel and floats
through the cut still holding a fragment; in the bleachers it passes Meg at eye
level while every person except her and the grape familiar rises gently.

VISUAL PROOF: Intimate matched close-ups; airborne lychee helper exits beside
Tina's cheek, enters beside Meg's; only peel fragments appear; Meg and the grape
familiar exchange a look while Raul and the football world hover behind them.

BRIDGE: STRETCH/fragment + connection. A small worker becomes the witness that
links two private events.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + constraint transformation / config 4 +
“product never shown whole” / helper and levitation trunks.

### C5 — feral — only the impossible shadows connect

DRAW: discarded peel becomes sky / duplicate Tinas drift apart / armor waits
weightless / canopy becomes scoreboard / joyful crowd. Constraint: show only
the shadow of the impossible thing.

RULE: The live-action worlds remain ordinary, but their shadows reveal the
unseen crossover: Tina's three airborne selves cast a rainbow-canopy shadow
that becomes the scoreboard arch, while weightless mecha parts orbit Raul only
inside his shadow.

VISUAL PROOF: Two bright documentary frames with impossible ground shadows;
Tina and the car look normal above three divergent flying silhouettes; Raul
looks unsuited while his bleacher shadow contains a complete exploded mecha;
crowd shadows cheer.

BRIDGE: REVEAL + unrealized possibility/recognition. The inner fantasies exist
as visible but not yet material layers.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + constraint transformation / config 5 +
“only the shadow” / doppelgänger and mecha trunks.

### C6 — feral — helpers unzip gravity

DRAW: helpers pull hidden seams / miniature houses rise / gummy spectators
float / rainbow canopy becomes scoreboard / documentary impossibility.
Constraint: protagonist never reacts.

RULE: Flavor-specific helpers find hidden zipper seams in gravity itself;
lychee helpers open the street so houses rise around Tina, and mango/grape
helpers continue the same seam along the bleachers so gummy spectators float.

VISUAL PROOF: One impossible documentary match pair; a dotted line of tiny
helpers visibly pulls a seam through pavement and aluminum seats; everything

their candy and never react.

BRIDGE: PEEL/REVEAL + mischief and release. The product's tiny workers expose
gravity as another peelable layer.

LINEAGE: morphological collision + constraint transformation / config 6 +
“protagonist never reacts” / crew and crowd trunks.
