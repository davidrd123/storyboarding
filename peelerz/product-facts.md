# Peelerz — Product Facts (epistemic tiers)

The engine kept inventing wrapper physics for a gummy. This file is the
canonical material reference for all prompts, seeds, and renders. Tiers
matter: don't promote without evidence.

## CONFIRMED (David, source-level)
- **Both layers are edible.** The product is a fruit-shaped gummy: an outer
  skin that peels off a center. No wrapper, no foil, no paper anywhere in
  the object. The peel is candy — the peel is the first bite.

## RESEARCH-SUPPORTED (Amos materials / category reporting, via DMPOST_31)
- Fruit-shaped, multi-texture gummy; outer layer peels away from a
  **softer** center; marketed as interactive/multisensory; flavors incl.
  mango, lychee; "peel it or eat it whole" is the product's real choice.
- Ben's p.9 finale frame shows the fruit form: thick peel segments opening
  petal-like off a round core.

## PLAUSIBLE, NEEDS EYES-ON VERIFICATION (David: hold one)
- Molded seam/ridge where the skin parts (would give the "thumbnail catches
  the seam" device at any scale).
- Skin vs core finish: matte elastic skin / glossier core? (⚠️ conflict in
  sources: claude-chat says "solid gel center, glass-candy"; research says
  "softer center." Resolve by inspection before it drives a hero frame.)
- Exact peel behavior: tackiness, stretch distance, translucent thinning at
  the stress point, release wobble; the actual peel SOUND.

## CREATIVE EXTRAPOLATION (ours — legal, but never present as product fact)
- World-scale consequences: buildings stretch before peeling; peeled things
  are edible at any scale; peels-fall-up buoyancy; snackable world.

## Material vocabulary for prompts (derived from CONFIRMED + RESEARCH)
- **Stretch, not tear.** Everything peeled deforms before it separates:
  taut → thinning → translucent glow at the stress point → release with a
  wobble. Never clean-edged, never crisp, never perforated.
- **No paper physics anywhere.** No crackle, no foil glint, no sticker
  backing (render v02's white paper-backed sky corner violates this —
  gorgeous, but off-product; regenerate variant with gummy-membrane sky).
- **Two textures** = the product's visual signature: soft skin outside,
  denser translucent core inside (exact finishes pending eyes-on).
- **Appetizing always**: the peel must look like the first bite, not like
  packaging waste.
- **Confectionary, never anatomical** (Codex batch-02 finding): stretch-and-
  glow language can drift fleshy/body-horror at scale. Keep the material
  bright, clean, candy-lit — sugar-dusted, jewel-translucent, toy-glossy —
  especially on large membranes (canopies, films, world-scale peels).
